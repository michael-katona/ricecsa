Women's Group

CSC | Wed. 6:00p - 7:00p | Contact: Karen Martinez Perez (kmm10@rice.edu)

Join us for part two of two in this mini-series called Healthy Habits. During this transition period, it can be very useful to find some healthy habits (through exercise and meditation) early on. For this second session, join us as we take a meditative walk around Inner Loop and share what tools we use to assist our faith life. First 10 women who show up will also receive what I call an "emergency bag" with some things like tissues, bandaids, and more! It'll be a nice productive study break, so please come and bring a friend :)

Divine Office & Adoration

St. Mary's Chapel | Thr. 6:30p - 7:30p | Contact: Michael Katona (katona@rice.edu)

Come learn how to pray the Divine Office – a series of daily prayers which, along with the Mass, constitute the public prayer life of the Church. We will be praying the Vespers (evening prayer), followed by Adoration. If you are able, please download the iBreviary app to follow along with the hymns, antiphons, and readings.
